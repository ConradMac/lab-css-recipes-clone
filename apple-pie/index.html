Apple pie

This was my grandmothers's apple pue recipe. I have never seen anoterh one quite like it. It will always be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competition. I hope it becomes one of your favorite as well !

Ingredients

  • - 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • - 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • - 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • - 1/4 cup water
  • - 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • - 1/4 cup water
  • - 1/2 cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 425 degrees F(220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste. Add water, white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  2. Place to bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not run off.
  3. Bake 15 minutes in the preheat over. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
